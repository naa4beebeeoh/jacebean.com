About

Jace Bean is a visual artist whose journey began with a fascination for color and form. Embracing abstract expressionism, Jace explores the interplay of emotion and geometry, drawing inspiration from urban landscapes and the chaos of everyday life. Each piece is a vibrant reflection of curiosity, spontaneity, and the beauty found in abstraction.
